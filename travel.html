Travel

Our school trip to Budapest was an incredible experience, blending history, culture, and education. We explored iconic sites like Buda Castle, the Parliament Building, and the thermal baths, which gave us a deep appreciation of Hungary's rich heritage. The city's picturesque views of the Danube River and its bustling markets added to the charm, making our learning adventure unforgettable.

In Prague, we experienced a different kind of magic with the city's fairy-tale charm. We wandered through the historic Old Town, crossed the iconic Charles Bridge, and explored the majestic Prague Castle. The city's blend of Gothic, Renaissance, and Baroque architecture created a mesmerizing backdrop for our educational adventure.

Our school trip to Belgrade was an exciting adventure through the vibrant capital of Serbia. We explored the historic Belgrade Fortress, which offered stunning views of the confluence of the Sava and Danube rivers. Walking through the bustling streets of Knez Mihailova and visiting the impressive Saint Sava Temple gave us a deep appreciation for the city's rich cultural and historical heritage.
